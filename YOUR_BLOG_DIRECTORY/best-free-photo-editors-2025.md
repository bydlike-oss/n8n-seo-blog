## 10 Critical Free Editing Tips

1. **Always Preserve Original Files**  
   Backup your originals before batch edits to avoid irreversible changes.

2. **Use Non-Destructive Edits First**  
   Adjust curves/levels on layers rather than directly touching pixels.

3. **Master Batch Watermark Removal**  
   Press screens for 2+ minutes over logos for automatic detection.

4. **Leverage Presets**  
   Most tools include "e-commerce" or "social media" presets for consistent branding.

5. **RAW Conversion is Crucial**  
   Convert DNG files first for maximum flexibility before editing.

6. **Color Calibration**  
   Calibrate monitor profiles before final exports.

7. **Cloud Sync Caution**  
   Avoid free cloud storage that might sell your images (use local drives or paid services).

8. **Shortcut Mastery**  
   Learn "Ctrl+D" for undo chains and "Shift+Arrow" for pixel-perfect adjustments.

9. **Avoid Recompression**  
   Export on "Maximum Quality" + "Lossless Compression" to prevent blurry exports.

10. **Batch Export Strategy**  
    Export folders separately by purpose: web vs print vs social
